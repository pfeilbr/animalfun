Animal Fun is a simple animal learning program for children. Children learn about animals by seeing and hearing the sounds an animal makes. It combines an easy-to-use interface and fun sound effects to entertain children while they learn.

Features

  • 50+ animals
  • touch/tap to change animal
  • hear the animal name, the animal sound, or the animal name spelled
  • individual letters are highlighted as the animal name is being spelled
  • set the default sound when the animal is first shown

Sound Notes

  • Please make sure the PHONE IS NOT IN SILENT MODE (the switch on the side of the phone should be up) and Settings | General | Sound Effects is set to BOTH.
  • You need to use headphones or an external speaker on a first generation iPod Touch (as it has no external speaker)

Info

The animal pictures and sound effects are from the free Tux Paint desktop (Mac, Windows, Linux) drawing program for children. It can be downloaded for free at http://tuxpaint.org
